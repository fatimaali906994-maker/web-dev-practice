Our Story

"Where everyday comfort meets a touch of royalty."

The Crumb & Crown Journey

Born from a love of baking and the belief that simple ingredients can create meaningful moments, Crumb & Crown started as a small passion. Today, we share warmth, comfort, and care through freshly baked goods rooted in tradition and crafted with intention.

We bake fresh, handcrafted breads and sweets using quality ingredients, golden crusts, and timeless recipes—because every bite deserves to feel like a treat fit for a crown.

Mission & Vision

Mission: We bake happiness! To deliver high-quality, affordable, and innovative bakery items made with love and freshness.

Vision: To become the most-loved neighborhood bakery by serving creative and irresistible baked goods that make every day sweeter.

Business Strategy

Revenue Streams: In-store sales, Custom cakes, Bulk event orders, and Home delivery.

Financial Goals: Maintaining high profit margins through daily fresh production and bulk purchasing to reduce waste and per-unit costs.
